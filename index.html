About the Club

The Commercium Club, launched under the Department of Commerce at Maulana Azad College, serves as a platform for students to explore diverse business topics beyond the classroom. With a focus on experiential learning, leadership, and creativity, the club organizes impactful events that promote inter-college engagement and academic enrichment.

Our Events

We host a variety of academic and professional development events including:

  • Business case competitions
  • Commerce-themed quizzes and workshops
  • Keynote sessions with industry experts and alumni
  • Inter-college fests and collaborative forums

Our Vision

The Commercium Club aims to create a lasting academic platform for commerce students, enabling leadership, creativity, and industry engagement. With strong support from the Department of Commerce, the club bridges classroom knowledge with real-world business exposure and peer-driven growth.
